Implementation and Visualization of Numerical Algorithms

Numerical Analysis is the area of Mathematics that deals with creating, analyzing and studying Mathematical problems with the help of computers. Numerical Analysis has a wide variety of real-world applications throughout the natural sciences, social sciences, engineering, medicine, and even in business. I feel that that for a student to thoroughly understand the course, it is essential that they can visualize and "see" the formulas and algorithms that they learn about in class. And with the current growth of computers
All the algorithms have been written using Python and this website is hosted on Heroku
